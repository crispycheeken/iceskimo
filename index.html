WHAT IS SHAVED SNOW?

FLUFFY GOODNESS


Iceskimo specializes in Taiwanese-inspired shaved ice or snow.

Snow is a refreshing treat that is lighter than ice cream and creamier than shaved ice.

ICESKIMO'S PRODUCTS

HOW IT WORKS


Choose a cup size, shaved snow flavor(s), and toppings. Pay by the ounce and enjoy!

Iceskimo uses the the finest ingredients to handcraft our flavors and toppings to give you the best shaved snow experience.

Iceskimo's Matcha Green Tea and Original Milk shaved snow: a match made in heaven.

Original Milk shaved snow - featuring freshly cut strawberries and kiwis!

Mix it up! Add your favorite toppings and drizzles inside of your snow to create a custom flavor mash-up, like Nutella Chocolate Pretzel.

HOW WE MAKE SNOW

OUR PROCESS


Using only the finest ingredients, natural flavors are infused into ice blocks and shaved using a custom machine.

No powders. No syrups. No artificial sweeteners. We believe in the goodness of pure, simple foods to serve you the best shaved snow.
